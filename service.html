FAQ's

What is the Klutch Method?

  • The Klutch Method is a series of unique techniques used to command respect and obedience from your dog. You become the alpha your dog has been waiting for. We touch on this method in every class as it is our signature training that sets us apart from the rest of Seattle's Dog Training Businesses. This method will work if you put in the time and effort. Let us show you how to take control!

Do you do house visits?

  • We have a few trainers that do house calls. Reach out to us to set up your custom training package. We reccommend this for folks who don't have the ability to come to our locaiton or they just don't have the time. No matter what, we've got you covered!

Is all your training done in groups?

  • All of our classes are done in groups as we believe this is a great opportunity to socialize your dog.

Do you offer private training on site?

  • Yes! Reach out to us to set up your personalized private training today. We recommend this for dogs who need behavioral correction training. Look below for a list of all the different skills we teach.
